Privacy policy

Privacy policy.

MotionSelect uses enquiry information only to understand the application and respond to the person or company that contacted us. Enquiries are treated as confidential and are not shared with vendors for lead generation.

// Sections

01 / Information we receive 02 / How we use it 03 / Sharing 04 / Retention 05 / Your rights 06 / Contact

Information we receive

When you contact MotionSelect through the website or by email, we may receive the information you choose to provide, including your name, work email, company, project context, application description, technical requirements, selected service, timeline and budget range.

We do not ask for unnecessary personal information. The purpose of the enquiry is technical and commercial qualification, not consumer profiling.

How we use it

We use enquiry information to:

  • understand the motion, automation or control-system requirement;
  • reply to your enquiry by email;
  • decide whether the request fits MotionSelect’s scope;
  • prepare a follow-up discussion or proposal if relevant.

At this stage, MotionSelect does not operate a marketing database from enquiry submissions. We respond to the enquiry and handle the conversation directly.

Sharing

We do not sell enquiries or leads to suppliers. Information submitted to MotionSelect is not shared with vendors for paid lead generation.

If a project later requires an introduction to a supplier, integrator or specialist, this would only be done as part of the agreed engagement and with the context made clear.

Retention

Enquiry information may remain in normal email records for as long as needed to handle the request, maintain business records, or protect legitimate business interests.

If the enquiry does not progress, we do not use it for unrelated marketing activity.

Your rights

You can ask us to confirm what information we hold about your enquiry, correct inaccurate information, or delete enquiry information where there is no continuing reason to keep it.

Some information may need to be retained where required for legitimate business, legal, accounting or dispute-related reasons.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reply to the email conversation you have with MotionSelect or contact us through the website application form.

This policy may be updated as MotionSelect’s operating model develops.

Last updated: April 2026. This page is written to describe the current MVP operating model: enquiry received, confidential review, reply by email.
